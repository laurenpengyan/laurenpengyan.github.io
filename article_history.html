History

The work on WebDAV started in year 1996 when a phd graduate started working with world wide web consortium to discuss the problems in WWW(world wide web)'s problems of distributed authoring.WWW was made for both reading and editing web pages but as time grew it basically became only read only medium. The group decided to separate the work of distributed authoring and version control.It left the work of version control for fututre and concluded its work in 2007
