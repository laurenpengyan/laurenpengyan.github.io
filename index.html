Welcome to the Gate to Japan!

Japanese culture is unique while interesting. It combines the best of the western and eastern culture. Japan is a land of incredible contrasts - from neon skylines and ancient castles to serene shrines and speeding bullet trains, a tour of the Land of the Rising Sun never fails to fascinate. With us, travel to Japan in style, staying in the country's finest hotels and enjoying insider access opportunities rarely available to the ordinary traveller.

Upcoming Events

There are a lot of events about the Japan around the world.

Saturday, Dec 22 — Sunday, Dec 23

Christams Event

Another offering from BMI, an organisation responsible for some of the more generic festivals that take place in Yoyogi over the course of the year. This one is no different, with belly dancing, samba, and cuisine from Southeast Asia making an appearance in the past. Not the most authentic of Tokyo’s Christmas Markets, but once you’ve got a couple of glasses of mulled wine swirling around inside you who really cares? The event runs from 10am to 7pm on both days.

Saturday, Dec 22 | 7:00 PM — 7:10 PM

Odaiba Rainbow Winter Fireworks 2018

Almost 2,000 fireworks will be launched over 10 minutes at 7pm every Saturday in December from Daiba Park, as well as the area near Odaiba’s Statue of Liberty. The display just before Christmas will also be choreographed to music. You’ll need to wrap up warm, but the clear winter skies make for quite a display.
