Welcome to Lauren Elementary School

This is the Lauren school,which located in Daly city. In an effort to encourage continuing improvement in technology, interactive learning, and communication. Classrooms are equipped with state-of-the-art technological equipment which includes wireless laptops for teachers, Promethean Boards, document cameras,classroom web-pages, voice mail, e-mail, LCD Projectors and apple tv's. A computer lab is available for teachers and students to use to engage and promote the skills in the use of computer technology. Most of our classrooms have a complete set of IPADs for the students to use daily.
